# How to create a launch plan for an NFT?

1. Connect your wallet, then click the **profile icon** and go to **My Creations.**

   <img src="/img/launch-create/1.png">
   <img src="/img/launch-create/2.png">

2. Select **Launch** and click the **Launch** button.

   <img src="/img/launch-create/3.png">

3. Fill in the relevant launch information â€“ Step 1.

- Collection logo
- Collection name
- Collection description
- Maximum supply of NFTs in the collection
- **Royalty:** Once set, minted NFTs will automatically pay the creator a share based on the royalty percentage whenever they are sold in the future.

   <img src="/img/launch-create/4.png">

4. Choose the type of collection.

    **Sequential Minting:** All NFTs use the same image and are minted and displayed in order.

   **Random Minting:** Upload N images along with their supply quantities; the NFTs will be minted randomly.

   <img src="/img/launch-create/5.png">

5. Fill in the relevant information for the NFT.

   **Sequential Minting:** Only one NFT information entry is required.

   **Random Minting:** At least two NFT information entries are required; you can add more according to your launch plan.

- **NFT Logo:** Upload the image for the NFT display
- **NFT Name:** Enter the name of the NFT
- **NFT Description:** Enter a description for the NFT
- **Quantity:** Enter the supply quantity for this NFT

    <img src="/img/launch-create/6.png">

6. Set the stages of the launch.

   You can set the stages according to your own launch plan:

- **Stage Name:** Currently includes Whitelist Stage, Presale Page, and Public Sale Stage
- **NFT Price for This Stage:** You can set it as free minting, in which case the Gas fee will be paid by the creator; or set a limited price for minting

    <img src="/img/launch-create/7.png">


7. Once the launch plan is successfully created, you can go to the launch list to view details and initiate the designated launch.

    <img src="/img/launch-create/8.png">
