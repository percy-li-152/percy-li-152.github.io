# How to initiate a designated launch for an NFT launch plan?

1. In the **Launch** section, find the corresponding collection.

   <img src="/img/launch-initiate/1.png">

2. Click the **launch icon** on the collection.

   <img src="/img/launch-initiate/2.png">


3. Locate the stage you want to go live and click **Details.**

   <img src="/img/launch-initiate/3.png">

4. Fill in the corresponding launch information:

   **Whitelist Stage:**

- Whitelist addresses
- Start time and end time

  **Other Stages:**


- Start time and end time


   <img src="/img/launch-initiate/4.png">
