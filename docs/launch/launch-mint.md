# How to participate in the minting of a launch project?

1. Find the collection you are interested in and locate the corresponding **Launch** tab.

   <img src="/img/launch-mint/1.png">

2. Check the current stage of the collectionâ€™s launch and confirm whether you are eligible to participate.
   (For the whitelist stage, only qualified addresses are allowed to mint the NFT.)

   <img src="/img/launch-mint/2.png">

3. Enter the number of NFTs you want to mint and sign with your wallet to successfully complete the minting.

   <img src="/img/launch-mint/3.png">

4. After successful minting, you can go to **My Assets** to view the NFTs you have successfully minted.

   <img src="/img/launch-mint/4.png">
