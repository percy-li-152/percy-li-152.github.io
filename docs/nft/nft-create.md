# How to create NFTs?

On Goldust, you can quickly and smoothly create your own exclusive art NFTs and sell your works on the platform. Now, let's start creating together!


## Choose Collection

1.  Connect your WEB3 wallet.
    <img src="/img/nft-create/choose-collection/1.png" style="width: 100%">

2.  Click on your profile icon and select the **"My Creations"** option.
    <img src="/img/nft-create/choose-collection/2.png" style="width: 100%">

3.  In "My Creations," click on **"Create Items."**
    <img src="/img/nft-create/choose-collection/3.png" style="width: 100%">

4.  Choose an existing "Collection" or create a "New Collection."
    <img src="/img/nft-create/choose-collection/4.png">

5.  After selecting a collection, choose" Single Creation" or "Batch Creation".


## Single Creation

1.Upload the file of the NFT you want to create.
    <img src="/img/nft-create/single-creation/1.png" style="width: 100%">

2.  Edit the relevant information of the NFT, including the name, description, and attributes.
    <img src="/img/nft-create/single-creation/2.png">

3.  After confirming the information, finalize the creation and sign with your wallet.

4.  Once the creation is successful, you can go to "Assets" to view the details.


## Batch Creation

1.  Bulk upload multiple NFT files.
    <img src="/img/nft-create/batch-creation/1.png" >

2.  Edit the relevant information of the NFTs, including the name, description, and attributes (you can edit them in bulk or individually for customization).
    <img src="/img/nft-create/batch-creation/2.png" >

3.  After confirming the information, finalize the creation and sign with your wallet.

4.  Once the creation is successful, you can go to "Assets" to view the details.
